We’re local.

We’d like to meet you for coffee or lunch. Talk about your questions, your life, the details no large insurer understands. We care about you, and the family that will remain on the other side of the policy.

The transactional model in this category goes: place the policy, collect the commission, lose touch until renewal or claim. We work the other way. The work is finished when the policy pays — which means showing up at placement, through the life events that shift a family’s planning, and at the hospital on the day a beneficiary has to claim the benefit.

The firm is based in Encinitas and serves the North County coastal corridor and greater San Diego.
